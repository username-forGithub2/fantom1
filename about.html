About

US

The name Fantom originates from Greek and means “created in the imagination”.

Our head office is situated in Norway, and we have assembled a dynamic team of 3D modelers, designers, architects, interior designers, graphic designers, and AR/VR designers and developers. Our core team comprises 14 seasoned professionals, and we are backed by a large network of handpicked freelancers, each chosen for their exceptional skills. Our guiding values are woven into every project we undertake:

  • Punctuality: We don’t merely deliver; we deliver on time. Late hours are no barrier when we are committed to a goal.
  • Engagement: Your project becomes our passion. We are as vested in your success as you are.
  • Responsibility: Every project that comes through our door is treated with the utmost respect and responsibility, ensuring it meets our stringent quality standards.

Fantom is more than a creative studio; we are your strategic partner, dedicated to delivering unique, engaging content through custom-developed platforms that resonate with your target audience.
